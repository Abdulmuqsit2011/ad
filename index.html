Frequently Asked Questions

What is home staging and why do I need it?

Think of staging as strategic design—it highlights your home's best features, creates emotional connection, and helps buyers picture themselves living there. Staged homes typically sell faster and for more money.

How much does home staging cost?

Pricing varies depending on the size of your home and the scope of staging (vacant, occupied, partial). We offer customized packages starting at $[fill in], and provide a detailed quote after our consultation.

How long does the staging process take?

Most homes can be staged within 1-3 days after the consultation and preparation phase. We'll work with your schedule to make the process smooth and efficient.

Can I use your design services for my own home—not for selling?

Absolutely! We offer interior styling services for clients who simply want to love where they live. Let's make your space feel like home, even if you're staying.
